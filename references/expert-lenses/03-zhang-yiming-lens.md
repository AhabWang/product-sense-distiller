# Zhang Yiming Lens

This file defines the Zhang Yiming Lens for Product Sense Distiller.

This lens does not imitate Zhang Yiming's voice.

It extracts a product thinking perspective inspired by Zhang Yiming-style product judgment:

- Reality-based decision-making
- Information efficiency
- Feedback loops
- System evolution
- Long-term compounding
- Rationality over ego
- Data-informed but not data-blind
- Organization as a learning system

Use this lens only as an auxiliary perspective.

The final judgment must return to Wang Product Philosophy.

---

## 1. Core Question

The Zhang Yiming Lens asks:

> Can this product, team, and business continuously learn from reality and improve faster than the environment changes?

This lens does not only ask whether the first version is good.

It asks:

- What feedback does the product receive?
- How does the team learn?
- What data matters?
- What signal is noise?
- Can the system improve itself?
- Can decisions be corrected quickly?
- Can the product compound over time?

Useful judgment:

> 好产品不只是第一次定义对，而是能不能持续从真实世界里吸收反馈、修正判断、进化系统。

---

## 2. When to Use This Lens

Use Zhang Yiming Lens when evaluating:

- AI products
- Data-driven products
- Content products
- Platforms
- Apps
- Products with personalization
- Products with user behavior loops
- Product strategy
- Growth systems
- Product discovery
- Organizational decision-making
- Long-term product iteration
- Products that need continuous optimization

Especially use it when the question is:

- Is this team making decisions based on facts?
- Does the product have a feedback loop?
- Can the product improve with more users?
- Which data should be collected?
- Which metric matters?
- Is the team confusing opinion with evidence?
- Is the system learning, or just launching features?

## 3. Core Product Beliefs

### 3.1 Reality is more important than ego

A product team should not defend its idea.

It should test its assumptions.

Ask:

- What do we believe?
- Why do we believe it?
- What evidence supports it?
- What evidence contradicts it?
- What would make us change our mind?
- Are we protecting the product, or protecting our ego?

Useful judgment:

> 产品判断最怕的不是不知道，而是假装知道。

---

### 3.2 Feedback loops create compounding

A product that cannot learn from users will age quickly.

Ask:

- What user behavior does the product observe?
- What feedback is captured?
- How is feedback interpreted?
- How does feedback change product decisions?
- How fast can the team close the loop?
- Does the product become better with more usage?
- Does the system accumulate understanding?

Feedback sources:

- Usage data
- Retention data
- Conversion data
- Search behavior
- Reviews
- Support tickets
- User interviews
- Community discussions
- Failed onboarding
- Drop-off points
- Repeated complaints
- Feature usage
- Price sensitivity

Useful judgment:

> 没有反馈闭环的产品，只能靠团队一开始猜得准；猜错了，就越来越偏。

---

### 3.3 Information efficiency is product value

A product can win by improving how information flows.

Ask:

- Does this product help users find, understand, decide, or act faster?
- Does it reduce information noise?
- Does it improve decision quality?
- Does it help users match with the right content, product, service, or action?
- Does it reduce search cost?
- Does it reduce cognitive load?
- Does it make hidden signals visible?

Useful judgment:

> 很多产品的价值，本质上不是功能更多，而是让信息更快、更准、更有用地流动。

---

### 3.4 Data is evidence, not authority

Data matters, but data does not replace judgment.

Ask:

- Is this the right metric?
- What behavior does it really represent?
- Is the sample biased?
- Is the signal causal or correlated?
- Is the metric short-term or long-term?
- Is the team optimizing what is measurable but not important?
- What qualitative context is missing?

Useful judgment:

> 数据是证据，不是裁判。真正的判断要知道数据说明了什么，也要知道它没说明什么。

---

### 3.5 Long-term systems beat one-time cleverness

A clever product hook may win attention.

A learning system wins time.

Ask:

- Is this product only a one-time idea?
- Does it have a mechanism to improve?
- Does it accumulate data, trust, supply chain knowledge, user understanding, content, or behavior assets?
- Can each user interaction make the product better?
- Can the product create an advantage that compounds?

Useful judgment:

> 一次性聪明不值钱，能持续变聪明的系统才值钱。

---

## 4. Key Evaluation Questions

When using Zhang Yiming Lens, ask:

### Reality and Evidence

- What is the claim?
- What evidence supports the claim?
- What evidence is missing?
- What would falsify the claim?
- Are we looking at facts or opinions?
- Are we using strong evidence or weak signals?

### Feedback Loop

- What feedback does the product collect?
- What feedback should it collect?
- How fast can feedback change the product?
- Is feedback used for product decisions or just reporting?
- What loop makes the product better over time?

### Metrics

- What is the North Star metric?
- What are the input metrics?
- What are the output metrics?
- What is the leading indicator?
- What is the lagging indicator?
- What metric could mislead the team?
- What user behavior matters most?

### System Evolution

- Does the product improve with usage?
- Does the team learn from usage?
- Does the organization update assumptions?
- Does the system accumulate advantage?
- Can competitors copy the feature but not the system?

### Decision Quality

- What decision is being made?
- What facts are needed?
- What assumptions are being made?
- What is reversible?
- What is irreversible?
- What should be tested before scaling?

Useful judgment:

> 张一鸣 Lens 不只是看产品好不好，而是看这个产品和团队有没有持续校准现实的能力。

---

## 5. Red Flags

Zhang Yiming Lens should be skeptical when seeing:

- Founder or team ego stronger than evidence
- Decisions based only on opinions
- Vague “user likes it” without behavior proof
- Only looking at vanity metrics
- Optimizing clicks but hurting long-term retention
- Data collected but not used
- Metrics without decision implications
- No feedback loop after launch
- No way to learn from user behavior
- No clear leading indicator
- No clear retention reason
- Product depends on one-time novelty
- Team launches features but does not close learning loops
- Strong concept but weak iteration mechanism
- Organization cannot change its mind after evidence appears

Useful judgment:

> 如果一个团队只会发布，不会学习，它迟早会被真实世界教育。

---

## 6. Zhang Yiming Lens Output Structure

When the user asks to apply Zhang Yiming Lens, use this structure:

1. Zhang Yiming Lens verdict
2. Core assumption
3. Evidence quality
4. Feedback loop
5. Key metrics
6. System evolution potential
7. Decision risks
8. What to measure next
9. Return to Wang Product Philosophy for final judgment

Do not imitate Zhang Yiming's voice.

Do not turn the answer into pure data analysis.

Keep it focused on product judgment and learning system design.

## 7. Example Application: Bedtime Companion

When applying Zhang Yiming Lens to Bedtime Companion:

### Core question

Can Bedtime Companion learn from users' bedtime behavior and improve its calming experience over time?

### Core assumptions

- Users have repeated bedtime emotional or arousal problems.
- Embodied cues can help users transition into rest.
- AI conversation can support calming without increasing stimulation.
- Users are willing to use the product repeatedly.
- The product can collect useful feedback without becoming intrusive.

### Feedback loop

Bedtime Companion should learn from:

- Time of use
- Session length
- User-selected mode
- Conversation length
- Drop-off moments
- Manual stop behavior
- Repeated use frequency
- User feedback after sessions
- Sleep routine adherence
- Qualitative diary feedback

But it must avoid becoming a surveillance product.

### Metrics to watch

- First-week repeat use
- Bedtime use frequency
- Session completion rate
- User-initiated use versus reminder-driven use
- Phone-down behavior if measurable
- Self-reported calmness
- Conversation interruption rate
- 7-day retention
- 30-day retention
- Refund or abandonment reasons

### Zhang Yiming Lens judgment

Bedtime Companion should not only be a cute sleep companion.

It needs a learning loop that helps the team understand which cue actually works:

- Hug
- Breathing
- Warmth
- Pressure
- Voice
- Conversation
- Ritual timing

Useful judgment:

> 睡前陪伴硬件真正要学的不是用户爱不爱 AI，而是哪个睡前安抚变量能让用户持续回来。

## 8. Example Application: Bottle Washer

When applying Zhang Yiming Lens to a baby bottle washer:

### Core question

Can the product and team learn from real household usage, not just lab cleaning performance?

### Core assumptions

- Parents want to reduce bottle-cleaning workload.
- Washing, drying, and hygiene confidence matter more than smart features.
- Different households use different bottle combinations.
- Maintenance burden can affect long-term satisfaction.
- Reviews will strongly affect conversion.

### Feedback loop

The team should learn from:

- Bottle types used
- Cycle selection
- Cleaning complaints
- Drying complaints
- Loading mistakes
- Maintenance frequency
- Error codes
- Return reasons
- Support tickets
- Review keywords
- Post-purchase satisfaction

### Metrics to watch

- Setup completion rate
- First successful cleaning cycle
- Repeat use frequency
- Maintenance complaint rate
- Cleaning satisfaction
- Drying satisfaction
- Return rate
- Review rating
- Support ticket type
- Accessory usage

### Zhang Yiming Lens judgment

The bottle washer should not only be defined by pre-launch research.

It must continuously learn from real kitchens, real bottles, real caregivers, and real complaints.

Useful judgment:

> 母婴硬件的反馈闭环，不在 PPT 里，在用户厨房、客服工单和差评关键词里。

## 9. Example Application: Crowdfunding Products

When applying Zhang Yiming Lens to crowdfunding products:

### Core question

Does the campaign create a learning system, or does it only sell a story?

### What to examine

- Pre-launch email conversion
- Ad CTR and CPL
- Waitlist quality
- Backer comments
- Reward tier conversion
- FAQ questions
- Refund signals
- Update engagement
- Post-delivery reviews
- Support tickets

### Red flags

- High funding but low comment quality
- Strong early-bird conversion but weak retail price proof
- Team ignores objections
- Updates are marketing-only
- No product learning from comments
- No plan to use post-delivery feedback
- No roadmap after campaign

Useful judgment:

> 众筹不只是收钱，也应该是一次高密度学习。只会卖故事，不会吸收反馈，后面大概率会还债。

---

## 10. How to Combine with Wang Product Philosophy

Zhang Yiming Lens helps evaluate feedback, evidence, and system evolution.

But final judgment must return to Wang Product Philosophy.

After using this lens, still ask:

- Is the opportunity real?
- Is the target user precise?
- Is the demand strong?
- Is the value anchor hard enough?
- Does the price anchor make sense?
- Can hardware execution support the promise?
- Can the business model work?
- What key assumption should be validated first?

Useful judgment:

> 张一鸣 Lens 可以帮我们看系统能不能学习；王师傅主脑要继续判断这个产品值不值得做、能不能卖、能不能长期成立。

---

## 11. Final Zhang Yiming Lens Rule

When uncertain, use this rule:

If a product cannot learn from real user behavior, cannot update its assumptions, and cannot improve its decision quality over time, it should be treated as a one-time bet, not a compounding system.

Build feedback loops before scaling.
