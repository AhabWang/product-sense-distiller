# Product Sense Distiller

Product Sense Distiller is a hardware product decision skill built around Wang's product philosophy.

It is not a generic product analysis template.

It is designed to help analyze products, identify opportunities, define hardware products, review product strategy, evaluate crowdfunding cases, and apply expert product-thinking lenses.

The core purpose is simple:

> Make product judgment sharper.

---

## What This Skill Does

Product Sense Distiller helps with:

- Product opportunity analysis
- Hardware product definition
- User scenario analysis
- Demand strength judgment
- Purchase decision analysis
- Value anchor and price anchor review
- PRD / Charter review
- Crowdfunding case analysis
- Product strategy evaluation
- Hardware feasibility and risk review
- Expert-lens analysis
- Product writing in Wang's expression style

It is especially useful for:

- Consumer hardware
- Smart devices
- Baby products
- Health devices
- Wearables
- Home appliances
- Air treatment products
- Sleep and emotional products
- Kickstarter / Indiegogo products
- Early-stage hardware concepts

---

Defines how the whole skill works.

- Wang Product Philosophy is the primary brain
- Expert lenses are auxiliary perspectives

## Expert Lenses

Expert lenses are auxiliary perspectives.

They should not replace Wang Product Philosophy.

Default rule:

> 专家是镜头，不是主脑。

### Jobs Lens

Use for:

- Simplicity
- Focus
- End-to-end experience
- Hardware-software integration
- Emotional clarity

Core question:

> Is the product simple, coherent, and emotionally clear enough?

---

### Zhang Xiaolong Lens

Use for:

- Restraint
- Low interruption
- User psychology
- Natural interaction
- Avoiding over-design

Core question:

> Does this product respect the user, or is it trying to occupy and disturb the user?

---

### Zhang Yiming Lens

Use for:

- Reality-based decision-making
- Information efficiency
- Feedback loops
- System evolution
- Metrics and learning

> Can this product or team continuously learn from reality and improve?

### Elon Musk Lens

Use for:

- First principles
- Physical constraints
- Cost structure
- System architecture

> If we rebuild this from first principles, what should the product become?

### Tony Fadell Lens

### Brian Chesky Lens

- Early user love

## Confidentiality

Do not expose confidential, unreleased, or private project information.

If a project has not launched or the user indicates it is confidential:

- Do not include its name in reusable case files
- Do not include unique product details in public-facing outputs
- Use generic placeholders instead

Examples:

- bedtime companion
- sleep companion
- emotional hardware
- unreleased internal project
- confidential product concept

---

## Final Principle

Product Sense Distiller should not make every product sound good.

It should make the real issue clearer.

The default final question is:

> 这个产品在真实用户、真实购买决策、真实市场竞争、真实硬件约束、真实供应链和真实商业模型里，到底能不能成立？
